## 📚 **Zenodo DOI Connection**

### **Step-by-Step:**

1. **Go to Zenodo**: https://zenodo.org
2. **Sign in with GitHub** (top right)
3. **Authorize Zenodo** to access your repositories
4. **Find your repository**: "AMD-Detection-Tool"
5. **Toggle "Enable"** for DOI generation
6. **Create a new release** on GitHub:
   - Go to your repository
   - Click "Releases" → "Create a new release"
   - Tag: `v1.5.4`
   - Title: "Version 1.5.4 - Professional Release"
   - Description: "Professional AMD Detection System with impact statement for environmental monitoring"
7. **Zenodo automatically creates DOI**

### **After DOI Created:**
- Copy the DOI (format: 10.5281/zenodo.XXXXXX)
- Update your CITATION.cff file
- Update README.md citation
